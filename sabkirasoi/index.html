About the Initiative

With the resolve that no one loses their battle to hunger during the pan-India lockdown enforced due to COVID -19 pandemic, Indian Political Action Committee (I-PAC) is starting its new initiative Sabki Rasoi.

Sabki Rasoi is a virtual kitchen for people in urgent need of food for their survival. The first phase of this initiative aims to provide more than 15 lakh freshly cooked meals over the next 10 days (upto 1.5 lakh meals per day) by involving over 1,000 professionals and willing individuals across 20 cities in India.
